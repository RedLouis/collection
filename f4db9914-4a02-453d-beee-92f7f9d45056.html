Description

TACTICAL EXTRACTION

Arena Breakout: Infinite has meticulously crafted 5 distinctive maps - the Farm, the Valley, the Northridge, the Armory, and the TV Station - with each demanding a radical shift in raid strategy. Sprint through the tight Valley corridors for hit-and-run strikes or plan a tactical siege in the Armory’s block-by-block warzone. But pay attention, nature doesn’t play by your rules - everchanging weather in the Dark Zone can alter every extraction into a new desperate gamble. Outthink, outgun, and extract - before the zone swallows you whole.

HYPER-REALISTIC, EFFORTLESS MASTERY

Arena Breakout: Infinite respects the depth military fans crave while welcoming newcomers with the red carpet treatment. For the die-hard military enthusiasts, this is a love letter to authenticity. Our Ultimate Gunsmith System features over 900 weapon mods on over 20 attachment slots to allow you to create your ultimate custom weapon. For newcomers, we’ve built in quality-of-life features like one-tap loadouts that let you gear up in seconds. Arena Breakout: Infinite strips away frustration so you can focus on the loot ‘n shoot fun.

RUN SOLO OR WITH YOUR SQUAD

Arena Breakout: Infinite offers a variety of game modes for all operators - Tactical Ops, Solo Ops, Covert Ops and fast-paced 4v4 Deathmatch modes. Whether you prefer tactical teamwork or going in alone, you decide how you take on the fight.

TROPHY ROOM & PROGRESSION

The Trophy Room is your off-field stronghold, where every upgrade and decoration tells your story. That rare sniper rifle, a stack of high-tier armor, or a collection of enemy dog tags - showcase all your hard-earned loot with pride! Here, progression never stops and each season brings fresh challenges - complete weekly and seasonal tasks to unlock rewards that grow with your lethal reputation. The crown jewel? A free, top-tier 3X3 Titanium Case ensuring your most valuable loot stays safe no matter how tough the raid gets.

FAIR PLAY AND ANTI-CHEAT

In Arena Breakout: Infinite, fair combat isn’t just a promise - it’s a guarantee with our ever-improving, anti-cheat system. This includes Killcam Replays that allow you to submit a one-tap report whenever you flag suspicious activity. Moreover, we don’t just punish cheaters - we make it right for you. If you’re confirmed to have lost loot, gear, or a match due to cheating, automatic in-game compensation will hit your inventory. Last year alone, our anti-cheat system placed 10-year bans to punish over 123,000 players, and reimbursed over 630,000 players with over 400 Billion Koens.

CYBERPUNK 2077 COMING TO KAMONA

Kamona’s battlefields are about to get a neon-drenched makeover with the collaboration of the gritty, high-tech world of Cyberpunk 2077 crashing into Arena Breakout: Infinite. This isn’t just a simple crossover; it’s a full-throttle immersion into Night City’s cyber-dystopia recreated with pinpoint accuracy to satisfy die-hard fans and excite newcomers to the game. Collab tasks and Battle Pass are exclusively available during the limited-time event earning you themed loot that lets you carry Night City’s spirit into every raid.
